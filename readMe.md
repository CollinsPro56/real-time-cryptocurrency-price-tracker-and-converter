## Overview

The Cryptocurrency Tracker with Real-Time Currency Converter is a web-based application developed as part of the Industrial Training (SIWES) program to address the growing need for timely and accurate access to cryptocurrency market information. The system is designed to monitor live cryptocurrency prices and perform real-time conversion of digital assets into multiple fiat currencies.

The application retrieves up-to-date market data from reliable public APIs and processes this information dynamically to ensure accuracy and consistency. The data is then presented through a clean, user-friendly interface that enables users to easily interpret market trends and currency values.

The system provide the following core functionalities:

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->

- Display of current price for selected cryptocurrencies.
- Real-time tracking of price fluctuations based on live market data.
- Conversion of cryptocurrency values into various fiat currencies such as USD, EUR, and NGN.
- Provision of accurate and continuously updated financial information to support informed decision-making.
